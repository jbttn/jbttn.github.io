OR Connect Title & Escrow

OR Connect is the anywhere, anytime connection with Old Republic Title Company and ORTC.com for all of your title and escrow needs. This powerful smart phone/tablet application gives real estate professionals and consumers access to net sheets, a GFE calculator, title and escrow fees, closing costs, city transfer tax information, and provides instant information on who pays for what fees in each county. You can easily open an escrow, track all open escrows, view order history, view preliminary reports/commitments, contact your escrow officer or find an escrow office closest to your location.
